Re: Live
I got a good grade.
Today my cousins visited.
My dog died.
I reconnected with an old friend.
My little brother was born.
This site has been a brain child of mine for quite some time. Although it is FAR from being complete, I plan to finish it. The idea of Re: Live is to provide an online journal for people to record their feelings. The grid of circles are meant to record events (I couldn't figure it out) and after some time the user should see a grid of whatever color their memories are. The point is even if you have many blue circles and only one yellow circle the site is meant to remind the user that even if their lives have been filled with sadness for some time, there was a time when you were happy, and they can be happy again. I've seen family and friends suffer from depression and hopefully this site, when complete, can help them during the days when they don't know if they can feel again.
